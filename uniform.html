 School:
Nursery to Cl-V: Sky blue Frog with round-neck baby collar.

Cl-VI to Cl-X: Sky blue Kamiz with round-neck, White Salwar and scarf, white-folded cross dupatta & sky-blue belt. Kamiz length- below the knee. Sleeve length- minimum upto the elbow. Side cut of the Kamiz must be below the frill of the Salwar. (A picture of uniform to be added here)
College:
White coloured Kamiz with shirt-collar, White Salwar and scarf, white-folded dupatta & white belt. Kamiz length- below the knee. Sleeve length- upto the elbow. Side cut of the Kamiz must be below the frill of the Salwar. (A picture of uniform to be added here)
2 Winter uniform (01 Nov – 28 Febru) Navy blue coloured pullover sweater (full sleeve) and a tie as per the sample of the institution. Other instructions are as it is. (A picture of uniform to be added here) Navy blue coloured Kardigan (full sleeve) as per the sample of the institution. Other instructions are as it is. (A picture of uniform to be added here)
3 Shoe & socks Black Oxford Shoe & White Socks as per the sample of the institution. (Standard size Socks.) Black Shoe & White Socks as per the sample of the institution. (Standard size Socks.)
4 Belt Standard Black-coloured Belt. Belt-colour of the Kameez.
5 Monogram Pocket-Badge given by the institution. Badge on right sleeve.
6 Name tag Name Tag supplied by the institution to be tagged (with belku) on the right hand side of the shirt. Name Tag supplied by the institution to be tagged (with belku) on left dupatta.
7 Shoulder badge Shoulder-Badge based on houses given by the institution. Shoulder-Badge based on houses given by the institution.
8 Hair Recommended hair length for the boys is not more than 1 inch. For short hair two pig tails and for long hair braid / plait must be tied in.
9 others Students are advised to keep necessary amount of money with them and forbidden to keep unnecessary amount of money. Use of any types of ornaments and cosmetics is strictly prohibited. Female students willing to wear veil are permitted to use kamiz coloured veil with white scarf. Cell phone and other electronic devices are completely forbidden.
