Rules and regulation

Students are expected to maintain / obey certain rules and regulations set by the authority as discipline of the institution. Any Act that goes against shall amount to the violation of discipline, some are:-




1. Not being on time in presence.

2. Abstaining from any class or exam willingly.

3. Adopting unfair means in the exams or in the class tests.

4. Making indecent comments about the teachers and students.

5. Throwing any object to harm anybody / anything.

6. Scribbling on bench, chair, table, desk, blackboard, wall or in the toilet etc.

7. Lodging complaints in a body. But it may be done with the help of class teacher, house tutor or prefect.
8. Entering classrooms earmarked for the students of a certain class.

9. Causing damage to property or asset of this institution.

10. Grabbing bags, exercise books and any other materials of other students without their permission.

11. Not showing respect to teachers, staff, guardians, or senior students.

12. Not maintaining proper and reasonable distance among the male and female students.

13. Carrying any unnecessary books, leaflets, CD, mobile, MP3 player etc.

14. Carrying and keeping sports materials, wearing unauthorized dresses, caps, and ornaments etc.

15. Violating orders of any teacher.

16. Violating any rule of the institute.
