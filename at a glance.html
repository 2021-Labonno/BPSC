At a Glance

It is with pride that BEPZA Public School & College will take the responsibility of promoting education not only among the offspring of parents serving at BEPZA and the enterprises of Dhaka EPZ but also among the students residing in the surrounding area. With this motto in view, this institution was established on 15 December, 1999. Since the inception , the institution has been able to extend its range of service to a greater level starting with only 275 students to a current range of 2976 students.
