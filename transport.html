Transport


With a view to avail the transport facility, students have to fulfill the following compliances:
1. Permission for a seat in the bus

2. Collection of form from the office

3. Bus fare per month: Civil TK 1000/=

4. Payment of monthly bus fare is mandatory just after the date at which the permission for movement is granted

5. Submission of application at least 15 days ahead of cancellation of permission

6. No fare is to be charged after cancellation of permission

7. Notice of permission or withdrawal of permission will be served in the class and the students should note it down 8. Bus route information will be displayed on the notice board

9. Only those who reside adjacent to the bus route are eligible to apply for school bus.

10. If the permission for bus is granted before the 15th of a month, a student is to pay the total bus fare of a month; On the other hand, if the permission is granted after the 15th of a month, a student is to pay half bus fare

11. Permission for bus is not given if there is no vacancy of seats.
